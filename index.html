🚫 Site Blocker

Privacy Policy  ·  Last updated: March 24, 2026

Overview

Site Blocker is a Chrome extension that helps you manage your time by restricting access to specific websites during configured hours. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

Data We Collect

Site Blocker collects no personal data. The extension stores only the following information:

  • local Your block rules (site URLs, days, time ranges)
  • local Your language preference (ko/en)
  • local Global on/off setting

All data is stored locally on your device using chrome.storage.sync, which may sync across your Chrome profiles via your Google account under Google's own privacy policy.

Data We Do NOT Collect

  • No browsing history
  • No personal information
  • No usage analytics or telemetry
  • No data is ever sent to any external server

Permissions

Site Blocker requests the following Chrome permissions:

  • storage — Save your block rules locally
  • tabs — Detect the current tab URL to apply rules
  • webNavigation — Intercept navigation events to redirect blocked sites

Third-Party Services

Site Blocker does not use any third-party services, SDKs, or analytics platforms.

Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
